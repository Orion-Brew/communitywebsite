About Me

My goal is to be someone who people can look up to. The person I wanted in my life when I was younger. I'd like to inspire and show people experiences from my life up front, whether they be tragic, or blissful. I don't mean to be too sentimental but when I speak of my music, it brightens my spirit. Some of my biggest inspiration is my mother, my father, and the music that helped save me when I had my troubles. I want to continue to be able to share my songs. I intend to keep at that for as long as I have the time and funding. I had never touched an instrument before 2015 but now I play & record habitually as a form of meditation and for fun/excitment. Thanks to some of my amazing teachers I slowly began understanding the elements and bigger picture of music production and audio engineering. Some of these people include Odie Maddox, Effron Simon, Tony James, & Brock McManus. Still learning to this day I am always seeking new skills to improve as the journey continues.... Join me for this epic undertaking my friends.
